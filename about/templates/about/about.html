Our Begining and Motto

Inspired by our origins and passions for authentic traditional cuisines, we started and established the beloved Neupane’s Kitchen to introduce and provide homemade food. We collected experiences from 25 different countries to perfecting our tastes and crafting the menus of Neupane’s Kitchen.

Our main mission is to carefully prepare and cook healthy meals with various fresh ingredients which are sourced locally. In addition, Neupane’s Kitchen aim to have vast selections for vegetarian and vegan menus which promote “Go Green” and become an environmental-friendly business.

“Homemade gourmets for your taste, delivered straight from our kitchen to your home” is the message we would like to convey to our customers as we bring happiness to your dining tables and our delicious gourmets to be enjoyed together with your love ones as well as accompanied your various gatherings, parties and events.
